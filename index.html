Archi-II is in development

Archi-II has started development and is planned to be completed by mid-May. This model, with a large data and language base, is preparing to become Stoat.chat's most powerful AI assistant. As it comes from the Freedom team, it is expected to be quite sharp-tongued and critical.

New ranking system coming soon!

Users will be subject to an elo classification based on their message activity. Each inactive day costs -30 elo, while each message adds +0.5 elo. Ranks will be determined by elo levels, but these ranks are purely seniority-based and will not affect permissions.
